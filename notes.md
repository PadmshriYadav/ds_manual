* ## what are Infix Prefix and Postfix expressions 

* **Infix Notation (Human Readable)**
   ```<Operand><Operator><Operand>```
     Requires precedence rules (e.g., multiplication before addition) and parentheses to determine the unambiguous order of operations
     (A + B)*C (Parentheses force the addition first)
     A+B
     
* **Prefix Notation (Polish Notation)**
* **Postfix expressions**
 * Evaluates a space-separated postfix expression string.
 * Supports +, -, *, /, and $ (for exponentiation/power).
